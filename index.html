Wrestlemania Card

The following matches will be taking place at Wrestlemania 34.

*Card subject to change.

  • Brock Lesnar (c) vs Roman Reigns

    Universal Championship

    Roman Reigns earned the right to face Brock Lesnar for the Universal Championship by winning this year's Elimination Chamber.

  • Ronda Rousey & Kurt Angle vs Stephanie McMahon and Triple H

    Ronda took out both her opponents at Wrestlemania 31. She has since signed with Raw and Kurt Angle revealed the plan was to "get her back". Various brouhahas have ensued which led to Angle (who is also the General Manager of Raw) to make this match.

  • AJ Styles (c) vs Shinsuke Nakamura

    WWE Championship

    Nakamura won the 2018 men's Royal Rumble match granting him a title shot against a champion of his choosing. He immediately signaled his intent to challenge Styles, whomst he defeated at New Japan Pro Wrestling's Wrestle Kingdom in 2015.

  • Charlotte Flair (c) vs Asuka

    Smackdown Women's Championship

    Asuka won the 2018 women's Royal Rumble match granting her a title shot against a champion of her choosing. Despite being on the Raw brand she appeared after Charlotte Flair's victory at WWE Fastlane to instead challenge for Smackdown's women's title.

  • The Miz (c) vs Seth Rollins vs Finn Balor

    Intercontinental Championship

    Both Finn Balor and Seth Rollins have recent wins against The Miz, and both have challenged him, leading to a triple threat match.

  • Cedric Alexander vs Mustafa Ali

    Cruiserweight Championship

    This match will be the finals of a 16 man tournament organized by new 205 Live General Manager, Drake Maverick, for the vacant Cruiserweight Championship.
